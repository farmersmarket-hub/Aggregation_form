📖 Key Definitions

Aggregation Session
One complete route run — opens when the aggregator's PIN is validated, closes on submit. One parent record per route run, regardless of how many vendors are visited.
Scan Record
A child record under the session, created per vendor QR scan. Each scan captures validation of that vendor's load against what their Supply Chain Load Form promised.
Ad-hoc Driver
A substitute running the route who is NOT registered as primary or secondary aggregator for the business. Manual name entry required; routes to manager for approval.
PIN
Aggregator's personal numeric identifier. Validated against the aggregator assigned to this route in the Route Creation form.
